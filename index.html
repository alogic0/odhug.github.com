Odessa Haskell User Group (OdHUG) is young comminty, founded in may of 2012 which aims to unite Haskell ethusiasts in Odessa urban area, to encourage programmers use Haskell in everyday work environment. We are welcome programmers, researchers and amateurs to share their experince in monthly meet-ups, in friendly environment. As any growing community, OdHUG pursuing not only disscussion of different topics, but also gathering entusiasts to improve Haskell community environment via Hackatons.

If you're live in Odessa, visitng our beautiful city and have something interesting to share, or just want to listen, you are very welcome. Follow our group for more precise date of meet-ups and Hackatons.
